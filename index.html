Pondicherry

It is one of the beautiful vacation spots in the southern part of India. It is famous for its food culture and rich french heritage.

Activities in Pondicherry

There are many activities that can be performed in pondicherry. My favorite spot in order of my preference are:

  1. Water Sports
  2. Wine Tasting
  3. Exploring the Streets

Pondicherry is not just a tourism spot but also a food city with various types of dishes of food available street side and also in the famous restaurants of the pondicherry locality. Some of the famous dishes are:

  • Idly Sambar
  • Authentic South Indian Meals
  • Sea Food
  • Biriyani and many other dishes
